Education

When Typhoon Haiyan wiped out nearly 90% of schools in parts of the Philippines, some folks decided to get resourceful. Since 2013, the Philippines Foundation have managed to get 100% of local kids back into school by turning fishing boats into impromptu classrooms.

Health

The World Health Organization is using a new Health Equity Assessment Toolkit (HEAT) to monitor childbirth services across Indonesia. The software compares healthcare in different regions and with other countries to improve equal access to services for the poorest populations.
